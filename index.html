Welcome to Mars!

The first thing you must know is how to play Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock. We like to keep this under wraps off-world, but we make most of our decisions this way. We've created this tutorial to teach you the rules.

Funnily enough, we got "Rock, Paper, Scissors" from y'all eons ago, but you Earthlings never seem to have caught on to our* improved method. It's much better you know--fewer ties and more blood and all that. Why, we'd be insulted at Earth's refusal to adopt our improvements to the game, but we've come to realize that the rules may simply be too complex for the average Earthling, at least judging from the cognitive testing you put your supreme leaders through. What was it, "person, woman, man, camera, TV"? Sad.

*Note: the game was actually created by Sam Kass, a software engineer from Earth. The Martians are always stealing our stuff!

Instead of giving you the rules of game, we decided to create this little practice game for you. Each time you play a round against the computer, the relevant rule will display. Don't worry. If you want to keep playing after you've learned the rules, you can just reset the scores back to zero to start afresh. For now, you'll be playing against the computer. But just wait until you get to play against one of us! Better hurry up and learn, though, you'll be needing this skill throughout your travels--more than you know, my friend!
