Welcome to Mars!

My name is Gorge. No, not George--gorge--as in, "Yes I know I'm so gooooorgeous, and btw you're looking fab, too." Anyhoo, I'll be your tour guide while you're here.

The first thing you simply must know is how to play Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock. Funnily enough, though "Rock, Paper, Scissors" was imported from Earth eons ago, you Earthlings never seem to have caught on to our improved method, which results in fewer ties (and is fun with more players, too). We'd be insulted but we've come to realize that the rules may simply be too complex for the average Earthling, at least judging from the cognitive testing you put your supreme leaders through. What was it, "person, woman, man, camera, TV"? Sad.

But just wait until you get to play against one of us! Better hurry up and learn, though, you'll be needing this skill throughout your travels, more than you know, my friend!
